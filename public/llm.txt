# Prediqte

> Prediqte is a Reddit lead generation tool that helps SaaS founders find high-intent leads on Reddit in under 5 minutes using AI-powered relevance scoring.

## About

Prediqte analyzes your website and automatically finds Reddit posts and comments where people are looking for solutions like yours. It uses AI to score relevance and detect intent signals like recommendations, alternatives, and complaints.

Key features:
- AI-powered relevance scoring
- Intent detection (recommendations, alternatives, complaints)
- Competitor mention tracking
- One-time payment (no subscription)
- Results delivered in 2-5 minutes

## Target Audience

- SaaS founders (solo and co-founders)
- B2B SaaS companies
- Indie hackers and bootstrappers
- Founders looking for alternative lead generation channels
